Burple Nurples

Description

Burple Nurples are purple flower-shaped snacks that are constantly burping.

Ingredients

  • 1 schmegg
  • Cup of sugar
  • Two sticks of blutter
  • Snore-lander

Steps

  1. Gently crack one shmegg
  2. Add a cup of sugar
  3. Add two sticks of blutter
  4. Add some snore-lander
  5. Enjoy!
