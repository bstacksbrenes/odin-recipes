Clabbage Cobbler

Description

Clabbage Cobbler is a smelly cobbler that is made on Cartoon Network's show, Chowder. The dish is a traditional, Upper-Highland Marfield Forgenlegger dish that is prepared with many rituals before it is ready to be eaten.

Ingredients

  • Clabbage
  • Tears
  • Glumburger Cheese

Steps

  1. Stew up the clabbages
  2. Load clabbages into traditional Clabbage Smasher
  3. Add Glumberger Cheese
  4. Add Tears
  5. Perform traditional, Upper-Highland Marfield Forgenlegger dance (jumping on one foot while putting your finger on your nose)
  6. Enjoy!
